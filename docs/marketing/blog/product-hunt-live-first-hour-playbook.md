## Why I'm publishing this before the numbers come in

Because the process is what I can control. The results aren't.

Here's what I'm doing in the first hour of the Skissify Product Hunt launch, minute by minute, so that if you're launching something next month you can steal what works and skip what doesn't.

---

## 09:01 CET — PH goes live

**Step 1: Verify the listing**
Check: thumbnail renders correctly, gallery images all load, first comment is staged and ready, tagline reads cleanly.

**Step 2: Post the first comment immediately**
Staged first comment (the founder's "here's what it does" context note) should go in within 90 seconds of launch. This anchors the conversation before anyone else frames it.

**Step 3: Tweet the launch announcement (PIN IT)**
```
🚀 Skissify is live on Product Hunt — today, April 1st.

Yes it's real. We promise.

AI agents can now draw. JSON → hand-drawn sketch in under 2s. 
Free to try, no account needed.

↳ Vote if it's useful: [PH link]
↳ Try it now: skissify.com/editor

#ProductHunt #AIAgents #MCP
```
Pin this tweet immediately.

**Step 4: Tell 5 people personally**
Not a blast, not a newsletter. Five actual humans who might genuinely find it interesting. DMs, not @-mentions. Personal message, not copy-paste. This generates the first few authentic upvotes.

| Comments | 3+ authentic | 10+ |
| Website sessions | 200+ | 500+ |

| Editor tries | 20+ | 100+ |

The renders metric is the one I actually care about most. It means someone tried the thing, not just looked at it.

---

## What I will NOT do in hour one

- **Spam every community at once.** Staggered posting looks organic, performs better, and doesn't get flagged.
- **Ask friends to mass upvote.** Against PH rules, and it triggers their fraud detection.
- **Monitor metrics every 5 minutes.** Checking hourly is fine. Checking every 5 minutes is a panic spiral with no productive output.
- **Argue with skeptics.** Engage genuinely, offer the demo link, move on.
