## For Other Solo Founders Considering an April 1 Launch

If your product is genuinely useful, launch on April 1.

The filter theory is real. Skeptics become your best users because they self-selected for active curiosity.

The cost: probably 20-30% less press coverage than a normal date.
The benefit: higher-intent cold traffic and a built-in story.

For a bootstrapped product with no PR budget, that's the right trade.
