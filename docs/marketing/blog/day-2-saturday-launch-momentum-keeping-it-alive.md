## The Three Audiences I Didn't Plan For

I built Skissify for AI developers building MCP integrations.

In 34 hours, three other audiences showed up:

**1. Homeowners**  
Someone described their kitchen renovation to Claude, got JSON back, pasted it into Skissify, and printed the floor plan for a contractor meeting. "He understood immediately. Usually I spend hours trying to explain what I want."

**2. Dungeon Masters**  
Blueprint paper style + multi-harmonic wobble = looks exactly like a map from a treasure chest. The r/DnD community found this without any targeting from me.

**3. Geometry Teachers**  
One teacher generated 30 spatial math exercises in 10 minutes. The hand-drawn aesthetic "feels approachable instead of textbook."

This is what primitives do. You build a capability. Use cases find you.
